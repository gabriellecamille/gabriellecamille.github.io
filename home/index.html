Biography

Hello! My name is Gabrielle Ferreira, and I’m a master’s student in the Graduate Program in Communication at the Federal University of Paraná. I’m interested in cultural studies, reception, audiovisual fiction, television, gender, and Latin America. For my undergraduate thesis, I’ve investigated the reception of Turkish TV drama Fatmagul among Brazilian viewers. As a master’s student, I’m still researching Turkish TV dramas, now comparing the reception of these productions between Brazilian and Uruguayan audiences. Currently, I’m also a part of the research groups NEFICS e OBITEL/UFPR. Feel free to contact me if you want to know more about my work!
